About me

Hello!


My name is Anna Carlsson. Right now I'm studying my fifth and last year at Umeå school of Architecture at the master program Laboratory of Sustainable Architectural Production (LSAP) .

I'm born in Jönköping and grew up 20 kilometers outside in a town called Habo. When I was 13 years old we moved to Sundsvall. I went to Skvaderns Gymnasieskola where I studied Natural science. When I graduated the Upper Secondary School I either wanted to study medicine or architecture. But after designing and making a model of a family house (see project family house model) I decided that I really wanted to be an architect. So when I was eighteen years old I moved to Umeå to study further at the quite new Umeå school of Architecture.

During my spare time I like to exercise, hang out with friends and decorate my home. One thing I can't live without is chocolate!
