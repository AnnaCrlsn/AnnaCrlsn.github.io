Umeå School of Architecture
5th semester/autumn 2013
One month field work

Dharavi, Mumbai, India

During the autumn of semester 2013 we went on a study survey to one of the largest slums in the world, Dharavi, situated in India’s capital Mumbai. The aim was to map different areas according to a research topic. My topic became public transitions and I also investigated the informal market which later on became the focus in my bachelor's project, see Background to Bachelor's project and Bachelor's project: Informal market & community complex. In the beginning of the trip we also had some field work in Mumbai.
