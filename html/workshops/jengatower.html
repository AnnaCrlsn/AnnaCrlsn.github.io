Experimental structures

The Jenga Tower

With a starting point from my abstract model (top picture and right picture) we were supposed to create a structures that would enhance the structure of the model. The result became a movable structure that gives you endless opportunities to try different shapes and structures. We called it The Jenga Tower - A tool for structural investigation.

Brief

- Changing structure
- Investigation
- Overlapping system
- Locking system
- Cooperation
- Sliding joints

Group members

Anna Carlsson, Robert Garcia, Johan Nord and Matti Törngren
