Deployable structures

DTS - Deployable
Tent Structure

In this workshop we were tasked with creating a demountable or deployable structure. My group made one that we called DTS-Deployable Tent Structure which is a tent look-a-like structure that could be moved by a lorry.

Group members

Anna Carlsson, Andreas Häggblom, Johanna Lagersköld and Gustaf Ridderstöm
