Umeå School of Architecture
8th semester/spring 2015
Wood workshop

The Canon

We were tasked with creating a cantilever as long as possible by only using wood. The cantilever should be able to carry a 5 kg heavy bucket in the end (=50kN force if the cantilever is 10 meter). My group made a with-drawable construction that basically works as a canon. It can be up to 15 meter long but in the final test it could only be extended to 12 meter with the 5 kg bucket on.

Group members

Diana Barbuta, Anna Carlsson, John Grundström, Ida Huggert and Klara Nero
