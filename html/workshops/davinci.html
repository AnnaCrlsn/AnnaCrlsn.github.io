Umeå School of Architecture
8th semester/spring 2015
Optimizing structures

Da Vinci 2.0

In this workshop we were tasked with creating a bridge that should span 1,5 meters. Our provided material was small wooden sticks, some string and paper. In the end the competition was to test each groups bridge and count how much the bridge could take; the weight it could take divided by the weight of the bridge. Our bridge could take 23,1 kg, 31 times its own weight!

Group members

Anna Carlsson, Lars Huser, Therese Hägglund and Kataraina Jensen
