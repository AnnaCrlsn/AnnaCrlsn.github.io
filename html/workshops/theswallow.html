Umeå School of Architecture
1st semester/spring 2011
Experimental space workshop

The preparation of a
swallow like flight

With a starting-point in one of Italo Calvino’s text Hidden cities 3* from his book Invisible cities we created a 1:1 mock-up model that captured some spatial characters. After inputs the model changed successively and in the end people were invited to explore, experience and interact in our space.

*Own interpretive summary of Marozia

Marozia is a city in change, a revolution is about to happen. It is a tight fight between two different parts of the city; the rats that wants to stay by the ground, by the old city, and the swallows that wants to break free, towards something new. Even tough the swallows are putting up to a good fight the rats are always just a little step behind them...
